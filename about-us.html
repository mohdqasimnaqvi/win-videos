About Us

Alhamdollilah, Allah (s.w.t) gave us the tawfiqaat to successfully convey the message of Islam through our various activities during the past 23 years. World Islamic Network a registered Public Trust (under the Govt. of India) established in 1991 with the main objective of imparting true knowledge of Islam to people around the world. The greatest challenge as well as opportunity before the Shiite’s today, in this era of MEDIA, is to promote the teachings of Ahlul bayt (a.s). It is a fact that whatever role we have been playing in the propagation of Islam would have been impossible without the goodwill and co-operation of momineen. We say ‘JazakAllah’ to them and pray to Allah (s.w.t) to reward them abundantly for their good deeds.

Our Activities

  • PUBLICATION DEPARTMENT: WIN has published around 90 different titles, which are distributed FREE of cost around the globe.
  • WIN ACADEMY: Coaching Classes for Std. IX and X.
  • MOHARRAM CAMPAIGNS: Spreading the Message of Imam Hussein (as) through other Medias.
  • KHADIJATUL KUBRA LADIES LIBRARY: More than 5000 books on various subjects are available in this library.
